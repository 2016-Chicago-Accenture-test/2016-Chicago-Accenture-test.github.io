LitCity

Who we are

LitCity is the brainchild of four amazing teenage girls, all in highschool. We worked together on this project to bring attention to artistic spaces and events around Chicago, and made sure to let the users know its level of accessability, like if it has a wheelchair or ramp or if its near a bus stop.

My name is Yu Jing Chen and I am currently a junior in high school! I love being with people and having a good laugh. I'm always ready for a good pun or a cringe-worthy math or science pickup line. Those are the best ones:) I also believe in living life to the fullest and I'm hardly found sitting and doing nothing, although that is also very enjoyable! You can usually find me throwing around a frisbee or doing Zumba in the park. I also enjoy learning new things and love cool opportunities to try new things! I love moments, the ones in which you are smiling, or maybe crying, and just feeling and truly living. My favorite thing about art is that you can express these feelings and moments, and there are so many different ways and mediums to do so.
